Our Mission

The Cardano ecosystem is filled with clutter from unwanted tokens, harmful SCAMs, and trash NFTs. These pollutants poison your wallet and the environment. TKAN aims to solve this by creating a "Trash Kan" on the Cardano blockchain.

With TKAN, you can send any unwanted tokens or NFTs to our address, where they will be locked forever. Our smart contract ensures these tokens are disposed of responsibly, keeping them out of circulation and your sight.

How It Works

When you send unwanted tokens to TKAN's address, our validator locks them permanently in a specific trash can on the blockchain. The validator script ensures that these tokens cannot be moved ever again. This is how we protect the Cardano environment.

In exchange for a small fee (the same amount the SCAMMER sent you, with a minimum of 2 ADA), your tokens will be securely locked. If you send more ADA than the required minimum, you'll receive our exclusive FAN Token as a reward, showing your pride as a "LION of Cardano" who is helping to maintain a clean ecosystem.
